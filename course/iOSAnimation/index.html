How to create animation in iOS? A step by step video guide

iOS Animation Tutorial: Overview

By: Wes Chua Swee Kwang

Last update: 12 Nov 2020 · Online Video (20 hrs) · Intermediate

Tested on: Xcode 12.2, Swift 5.3

iOS Animation is an important part of your iOS user interfaces, as it is able to create special effects and attract the user attension to your apps. Moreover, animations are fun and enjoyable to learn because the thrill and feeling given to you when you clicked on a button or menu. You are able to see cool effect such as poped-up or move left or right. At the end of this course, you should know various iOS techniques such as UIView Animation, Core Animation, Particle Effects and more and able to create stunning animations such as transition, fly in, fly out, fading in, fading out and more!

In this course, you will watch the video on YouTube and follow through the topices in sequences to learn the skills and techniques needed. In each chapter, you will be given a starting project and work on the animation in this project. Of course, free feel to skip any topics if you know it. After the end of each topic you will be given a challenge to pratice the amazing animations!

Prerequisite:

In this course, you are required to know basic swift and iOS concept. If you are a completed beginner of iOS, this course is too advance for you as we will not be covering basic concept of Swift.

This course are arranged in sequential order from the easier to advance. For beginners, it is recommended to go through the topics in order since we will build up the fundemental knowledge you needed for the following topics. Also, remember you will be given a challenge at the end of each topic!

For advanced developers, free feel to use this as a guide and jump to the topics that you are interested to know.
